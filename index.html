Priyanka & Yashodhan Wednesday, 16 December 2015

Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favorite!

  • March 9, 2011

    He stole her heart

    Four years ago, Yashodhan asked her out. Elated by the offer, Priyanka said yes happily. And then began the journey unexplored. They spent time together on countless dates, little did they know what was next in store.

  • August, 2011 - August , 2013

    The Long Distance!

    One fine day, Yashodhan's admit to graduate school came. Happy and sad at the same time, they knew staying part was going to be tough. He went abroad while she stayed back. The bond between them started to grow stronger. Little arguments here and there, but they did sail (Skyped!) through.

  • August 2013

    From 7 oceans to none!

    Couple of years later Priyanka decides to go graduate school. Yashodhan is over the moon as he is going to meet her soon. Although the distance is reduced by thousands of miles, long distance devil continues to smile.

  • June 2014

    Are we there yet?

    Without any hesitation, Yashodhan popped the big question. Quickly Priyanka wore the ring with child's caprice and pressed on it a swift glad kiss. The bigger question was, is it time to tell parents of their courtship ? What if they say no, will this be the end of a wonderful friendship ?

  • July 5, 2015

    Engaged!

    Their families hit it off and things fell in place. With blessings from parents, relatives and friends, Priyanka and Yashodhan get engaged.

  • December 16, 2015

    D-day

    Wedding date is fixed and they will be getting hitched. Priyanka and Yashodhan want you to be a part of it. As they embark on their journey of love and friendship. So mark your calendars to celebrate their big day!
