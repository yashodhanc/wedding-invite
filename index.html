Priyanka & Yashodhan Wednesday, 16 December 2015

Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favorite!

  • March 9, 2011

    He stole her heart

    Yashodhan and Priyanka met at a college fest. After a couple weeks of daily chat and phone calls Yashodhan asked her out on a date. Overjoyed by the proposal, Priyanka said YES! They spent time together on countless dates, little did they know what was next in store.

  • August, 2011 - August , 2013

    The Long Distance!

    One fine day, while they were on a date; Yashodhan's admit to graduate school came. Happy and sad at the same time, they knew staying apart was not going to be fine. Even though they were far apart, there was a closeness that refused to subside.

  • August 2013

    From Seven Oceans To None!

    Two years later Priyanka decides to go to graduate school. Yashodhan is over the moon as he is going to meet his love soon. Although the distance is reduced by thousands of miles, long distance devil continues to smile.

  • June 2014

    Are we there yet?

    Without any hesitation Yashodhan popped the big question. Quickly Priyanka wore the ring with child's caprice and pressed on it a swift glad kiss. The bigger question was, will this friendship last if their parents learn of their courtship ?

  • July 5, 2015

    Engaged !!

    Their families hit it off and things fell in place. With blessings from friends, relatives Priyanka and Yashodhan got engaged.

  • December 16, 2015

    The BIG Day!

    Priyanka and Yashodhan have a request, they want you as their Wedding guest. So mark your calenders and save the date, because having you there would be great!!
